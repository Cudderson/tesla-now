What is a Candlestick Chart?


Candlestick Charts use 'OHLC' prices (open, high, low, close) to create 'candles' for a certain time interval, typically 1 day per candle.


Candlestick charts are a staple for investors, as they provide detailed insight to how the market is trending for a particular company, many companies, or the entire stock market.


Read more about candlestick patterns here: Wikipedia/candlestick
